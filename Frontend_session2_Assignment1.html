Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mis the eggs and cream in the bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making a omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
